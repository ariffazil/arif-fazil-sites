What is WAWA?

WAWA is your personal academic OS — powered by AI, protected by constitution.

WAWA was born from a simple observation: university students are overwhelmed. Between Moodle, email, WhatsApp groups, MS Teams, and Google Meet — information is scattered everywhere. Students spend more time managing their tools than actually learning.

We asked: "What if every student had their own personal AI assistant that knew their schedule, tracked their deadlines, and reminded them before they forgot?"

WAWA — Wide Academic Wisdom Assistant — built on the arifOS MCP constitutional AI framework, designed specifically for Malaysian university students. "WAWA" means "ours" in Swahili. Here it means: your own, personal, always-on academic companion.
