About This Project

🌱 How MERDEKA BELAJAR OS Started

MERDEKA BELAJAR OS was born from a simple observation: university students are overwhelmed.

Between Moodle, email, WhatsApp groups, MS Teams, and Google Meet — information is scattered everywhere. Students spend more time managing their tools than actually learning.

We asked: "What if every student had their own personal AI assistant that knew their schedule, tracked their deadlines, and reminded them before they forgot?"

Thus MERDEKA BELAJAR OS was created — built on the arifOS MCP constitutional AI framework, designed specifically for the unique needs of Malaysian university students. "Merdeka Belajar" — learning freely, independently, and without being constrained by broken systems.

Built as a university innovation project using the SCAMPER framework. Submitted as part of Business Ideation coursework — demonstrating how real-world problems can be transformed into viable AI-powered solutions.
