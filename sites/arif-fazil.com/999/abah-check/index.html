Would this make Abah proud?

The question that never sleeps

DEFINITION

Abah Check is not fear of judgment. It is a dignity-law fail-safe derived from Fazil bin Khamis — Arif's father, who died in 2024.

It is one of Arif's earliest and most sacred laws. It asks:

"Would the person who shaped my integrity be proud of this action?"

OPERATIONAL SCOPE

The Abah Check is triggered automatically by arifOS MCP when:

  • ✓ An action affects another person's dignity or livelihood
  • ✓ A decision is irreversible and high-stakes
  • ✓ The TEARFRAME gate fires AMANAH=LOCK
  • ✓ The action conflicts with a constitutional Floor (F1-F13)
  • ✓ Arif explicitly invokes it on any decision

WHEN IT FIRES

Every time the Abah Check fires, it is logged here — not to judge, but to preserve the record of a man who cared enough to ask the question.
