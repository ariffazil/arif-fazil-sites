BEKANTAN‑1

Malay Basin · Lead Interpreter
Problem

Determining the presence of stratigraphic traps in a mature, complexly faulted basin where traditional seismic indicators were ambiguous.

Uncertainty

High variability in reservoir quality and seal integrity. Geologic models were split between "low-risk dry" and "high-risk discovery."

Judgment

Pushed for a specific drilling location based on calibrated seismic attribute contrast, ignoring the "safe" conservative models.

Outcome

Successful discovery. Proved that stratigraphic trapping remains a viable play in mature basins when uncertainty is correctly bounded.

PUTERI BASEMENT‑1

Fractured Basement Play · Structural Lead
Problem

Targeting fractured basement reservoirs—an inherently chaotic and non-linear geological target.

Uncertainty

Fracture intensity and orientation could not be directly observed; models relied on regional stress field proxies.

Judgment

Mandated a "Hold" on drilling operations until triple-witness evidence (Seismic, Stress, and Analogues) were aligned.

Outcome

Discovery. Successfully intersected high-productivity fracture zones.
